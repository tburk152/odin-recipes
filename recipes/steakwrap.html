Steak Wraps

Bad to the Bone Steak Wraps

Ingredients
  • Freshly cooked steak of your choice
  • 1 Avicado
  • Steak easoning
  • Onion
  • Cheese of your choice
Preperation Steps:
  1. Cook steak on grill covering with steak seasoning.
  2. Chop up avicado and onion.
  3. Chop cooked steak.
  4. Add steak and remaining toppings onto a wrap and roll tightly - Enjoy!!
