Lasagna

Succulent Lasagna of orgasmic proportions!!

Ingredients
  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Meat (deer, beef, pork, turkey)
  • Cheese of your choice
Preperation Steps:
  1. Cook pasta noodles, drain and pat dry.
  2. Cook meat using salt and pepper, drain and set aside.
  3. Mix tomato sauce and seasoning, cook on low heat until it starts to bubble.
  4. Add meat to sauce and let simmer for 40 minutes.
  5. Place a layer of cooked pasta in bottom of baking dish.
  6. Put a layer of sauce mixture on top of pasta layer.
  7. Add a healthy layer of chees onto sauce micture.
  8. Repeat this process until dish is full or sauce / pasta runs out.
  9. Put dish into a preheated oven at 375 degF and cook for 1 hour (60 minutes).
  10. Remove dish and layer with another layer of cheese, let sit for 15 to 20 minutes.
  11. Plate healthy serving sizes and enjoy!!
