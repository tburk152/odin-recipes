Scorpion Soup

Succulent soup of body shaking proportions!!

Ingredients
  • Five dozen freshly caught Scorpions
  • Cream of mushroom Sauce
  • Curry Seasoning
  • Onion
  • tapioca flower (for thickening)
Preperation Steps:
  1. Cook scorpions in boiling water for 10 minutes.
  2. Add scropions to warm/simmering cream of mushroom soup.
  3. Mix in 3 tablespoons of curry powder.
  4. Add 1 full finely chopped onion to soup mixture.
  5. Simmer for 45 minutes, add flower as needed to thicken
